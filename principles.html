Principles
We invest where the mission and the market converge.+
The most durable defense companies are not built by chasing contracts — they are built by founders who understand the warfighter's problem and have the technical depth to solve it. We look for that rare combination: mission clarity and commercial instinct in the same team.
The procurement window is open. We move while it is.+
OTA authority, SBIR reform, and sustained pressure on legacy primes have created a generational opening for startups to win DoD business at speed. We invest at the moment of first traction — before a program of record, when the right partner and the right capital make the difference.
Attritable beats exquisite.+
Modern conflict has exposed a structural truth: scarce, expensive systems cannot be fielded at the pace and scale peer competition demands. We back companies building affordable, producible capabilities — systems that can be lost without consequence and deployed without ceremony.
Dual-use is the alpha.+
The strongest defense businesses are built on technology with commercial pull. We seek founders who can serve both markets — unlocking faster revenue cycles, broader strategic optionality, and a more resilient path to scale.
We are owners, not observers.+
We bring operating experience in defense acquisition, government contracting, and technology development to every partnership. We help founders navigate the customer, accelerate the contract, and build the team — because we have done it ourselves.
